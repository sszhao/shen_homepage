- choose a concise representation that matches human’s mental model while minimizing possible noise

- carefully analyze the subtasks and assign the most appropriate agent (human or computer) to complete them

- look for gaps and transitions, and try to design interface and interaction techniques to support transition

Overall Strategy

  • Identify tasks/user intention
  • Perform task analysis
  • List possible user states
  • Discover imbalance, directness, transition problems
  • Redesign the process/interface to overcome these problems
  • Implement & evaluate the new solution

Future

Computer is getting smarter & smarter
  • Computer can (sometimes) beat human
  • However, human + computer can often beat computer alone
  • In HCI, it’s important to study how to form the best human + computer teams
